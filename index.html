SKYLER HENNION

From an early age, Skyler was introduced to the world of technical theater through watching her mom who worked in the field as a scenic artist. By third grade, she began her own career in technical theater for her elementary school and continuing through middle and high school. To date she has worked on twelve productions. In her four years at Laguardia, she has concentrated in the building, designing, and fitting of costumes, and works as a dresser during shows.
